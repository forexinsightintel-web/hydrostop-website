Commercial EPDM Flat Roofing

We install and maintain EPDM rubber flat roofs on commercial and industrial properties of all sizes across the North West and North Wales — from small retail units to large warehouse complexes.

No hot works permit needed. EPDM is cold-applied — no naked flame, no torch, no permit required. We can work safely on occupied buildings without fire risk or insurance complications.

Commercial Buildings We Work On

  • Warehouses and logistics units
  • Industrial and manufacturing facilities
  • Office blocks — single and multi-storey
  • Retail units, supermarkets and shopping centres
  • Schools and educational buildings
  • NHS and healthcare properties
  • Hotels and hospitality venues
  • Housing association and local authority properties

Why EPDM for Commercial Roofing?

  • Large format sheets — fewer seams on large commercial roofs
  • No hot works — safe on occupied commercial sites
  • Fast installation — minimises business disruption
  • Can be installed over existing felt in some cases
  • 30-year warranty available on approved systems
  • 40-50 year lifespan
