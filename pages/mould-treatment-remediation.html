Professional Black Mould Removal

Black mould (Stachybotrys chartarum) and other mould species are a serious health hazard, particularly for people with respiratory conditions, allergies, asthma or compromised immune systems. Surface cleaning alone does not solve mould — the root cause must be identified and addressed.

Our Mould Treatment Process

1

Root cause survey

We identify whether the mould is caused by condensation, rising damp, penetrating damp or structural issues. Wrong diagnosis leads to mould coming back.

2

Safe mould removal

Using health-safe biocidal treatments, we kill and remove the mould from all affected surfaces. We use HEPA-filter equipment to contain spores.

3

Root cause treatment

We treat the underlying cause — whether that's condensation, damp proofing, ventilation or other works.

4

Prevention system

Where condensation is the cause, we may recommend PIV ventilation units or DMEV vents to prevent recurrence permanently.

Health Warning

Black mould produces mycotoxins which can cause or worsen respiratory conditions, skin irritation and headaches. If you have mould in your property, address it promptly — especially if children, elderly people or anyone with a health condition lives there.
