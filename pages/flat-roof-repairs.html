Flat Roof Leaking?

A leaking flat roof needs attention quickly — water causes damage to ceilings, walls, insulation and electrics fast. Water travels sideways once inside a roof build-up, so the wet patch on your ceiling is rarely directly below the actual source of the leak.

Active leak right now? Call us immediately on 0151 348 7088. We will get to you as quickly as possible and can provide emergency temporary protection.

Common Causes of Flat Roof Leaks

  • Failed or lifted flashing around roof lanterns or parapet walls
  • Splits or cracks in the membrane at upstands and corners
  • Blocked or failed drainage outlets causing ponding water
  • Felt blistering, cracking or delaminating
  • Failed sealant around penetrations — pipes, cables, vents
  • Storm damage — lifted membrane or damaged trims
