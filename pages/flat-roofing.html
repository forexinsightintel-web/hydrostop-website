What Is EPDM Rubber Roofing?

EPDM is a synthetic rubber membrane fitted over your flat roof in one large seamless sheet. Where traditional felt cracks, blisters and leaks within 10-15 years, EPDM lasts 40-50 years with almost no maintenance.

30-year materials and labour warranty on every installation — covering both the rubber membrane and the cost of any labour. Only possible because we are Firestone and Elevate approved installers.

Why EPDM Is Better Than Felt

  • Lasts 40-50+ years — felt typically lasts 10-15 years
  • One seamless sheet — no joints, no weak points
  • Cold-applied — no naked flame or hot works permit needed
  • Stays flexible in all temperatures — will not crack or blister
  • Does not support moss or algae growth
  • Extremely low maintenance
  • 30-year materials and labour warranty

What We Fit EPDM On

  • Garages — single and double
  • House extensions — kitchen, living room, orangery
  • Extensions with roof lanterns or skylights
  • Dormers and porch roofs
  • Bay window roofs
  • Garden rooms and outbuildings
  • Commercial and industrial buildings

Approved Systems

We are approved installers for Firestone Building Products and Elevate Roofing Systems. This is what allows us to offer the full 30-year warranty. A non-approved installer cannot offer this.
