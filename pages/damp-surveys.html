What Is Included in Your Free Survey?

Our free damp survey is a proper professional assessment — not a quick sales visit.

  • Full moisture meter readings across all affected walls
  • Visual inspection of internal and external wall surfaces
  • Identification of the exact cause — rising damp, penetrating damp, condensation or other
  • Assessment of any associated timber or structural damage
  • Full written report with findings, photographs and recommended treatment
  • Fixed-price quotation — no hidden costs
  • No pressure to proceed — completely no obligation

Honest, unbiased advice. Our surveyors are Federation of Damp qualified and do not earn commission. Our diagnosis is always impartial.
