Is Your Garage Roof Leaking or Looking Its Age?

We replace old felt garage roofs with high-performance EPDM rubber roofing — backed by a 30-year materials and labour warranty. Most single or double garage roofs are completed in one day.

Note: Do not just patch it. Patching a failing felt roof rarely lasts more than a year or two. A full EPDM replacement costs little more than repeated patching — and comes with a 30-year warranty.

Signs Your Garage Roof Needs Replacing

  • Water coming through the roof or down the walls
  • Felt blistering, cracking or splitting
  • Moss or vegetation growing on the surface
  • Sagging or soft areas in the roof
  • The roof is more than 15 years old
  • You have patched it more than once

Our Process

1

Free survey and quotation

We visit, measure the roof and assess the existing deck. Fixed-price quotation — usually same day.

2

Strip the old roof

All existing felt and failed material stripped back to the timber deck. Every board inspected.

3

Deck repair

Rotten or damaged boards replaced. Correct falls ensured for water drainage.

4

EPDM installation

Membrane laid, bonded and detailed around all edges and outlets.

5

30-year warranty

We clean up and issue your 30-year materials and labour warranty.
