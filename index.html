Frequently Asked Questions

What damp proofing services do HydroStop offer?

HydroStop offer rising damp treatment (DPC injection and replastering), penetrating damp treatment, basement tanking and waterproofing, black mould removal, condensation control and PIV/DMEV ventilation unit installation, and damp proofing plastering and rendering. All surveyors are Federation of Damp qualified.

What is EPDM roofing and why is it better than felt?

EPDM is a synthetic rubber membrane fitted as one large seamless sheet. Where felt cracks and leaks within 10–15 years, EPDM lasts 40–50+ years with almost zero maintenance. Cold-applied, flexible in all weathers, and HydroStop back every installation with an industry-leading 30-year materials and labour warranty.

What does the 30-year warranty cover?

Our 30-year warranty covers both the EPDM membrane materials and the labour cost of any repair needed on all flat roofing projects. This applies to flat roofing only — damp proofing works carry their own separate guarantees.

Is the damp survey really free with no obligation?

Yes — completely free, no obligation. Our Federation of Damp Qualified Surveyors visit, assess the problem, identify the root cause, and provide a written report with a quotation. No pressure to proceed and our surveyors do not earn commission.

Which areas do HydroStop cover?

We cover a 100-mile radius from Heswall, Wirral including Wirral, Liverpool, Birkenhead, Chester, Ellesmere Port, Warrington, Runcorn, Widnes, St Helens, Wigan, Bolton, Manchester, Salford, Stockport, Altrincham, Macclesfield, Crewe, Northwich, Southport, Wrexham, Flint, Rhyl, Prestatyn, Colwyn Bay, Bangor, Shrewsbury, Telford, Stoke-on-Trent and surrounding areas.
